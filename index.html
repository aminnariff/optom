Anterior Segment View

The cornea, iris, and lens are all parts of the anterior segment, which is the area of the eye that is closest to the face.

Posterior Segment View

The anterior hyaloid membrane and all of the optical components behind it are located in the posterior segment, also known as... the posterior cavity, which makes up the back two-thirds of the eye.

Vision Acuity & Therapy

An eye exam called a visual acuity test measures how clearly you can see individual letters or symbols from a distance.

Primary Optometry

Primary eye care represents the foundation of eye health. It emphasises the preservation of healthy vision and the prevention of eye illnesses.

Paediatric Optometry

A children's eye doctor, or paediatric ophthalmologist, diagnoses, prevents, and treats diseases that affect children's eyes and vision.

Ophthalmic Dispensing

Ophthalmic dispensing comprises matching optical prescriptions with the best eyeglass frames and lenses.

Binocular Vision

Binocular vision is the process through which the brain combines information from the two eyes to produce a single clear unified view.
